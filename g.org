* First Things First

Save the annoying customization barf elsewhere.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (setq custom-file "~/.emacs.d/custom.el")
  (if (file-exists-p custom-file)
      (load custom-file))
#+end_src

Free up screen real estate.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp

  (tool-bar-mode -1)
  (scroll-bar-mode -1)
#+end_src
